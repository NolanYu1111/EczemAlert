# EczemAlert

EczemAlert is a lightweight web app that helps people with eczema track flare-ups, identify potential triggers, and keep a visual timeline of their skin's condition over time. Users can log symptoms, upload photos of affected areas, and prepare clear summaries to share with their doctor.

**Who it's for:** People managing chronic eczema (and their caregivers) who want an easy way to spot patterns between triggers (food, weather, stress, products) and flare severity, and to communicate that history effectively during dermatology visits.

**Purpose:** Help users understand and manage their eczema by tracking symptoms, triggers, and progress in one simple place.

This project is a submission for **CAC 2026**.

## Features (Planned)
- **Home** – quick overview and shortcuts to log a new entry
- **Upload** – add photos of affected skin areas
- **Log** – record symptoms, severity, and possible triggers
- **Timeline** – view flare-up history over time
- **Doctor Check** – generate a summary to share with a healthcare provider
